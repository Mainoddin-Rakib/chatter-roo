Meet Your Therapist

Aneeqa Shah, M.A., CCC-SLP

Owner, Speech-Language Pathologist

Aneeqa is a pediatric speech therapist that is passionate about working with young children and their families. After working with children in the public schools and private clinics, Aneeqa was inspired to open Chatter-Roo Speech and Language Therapy, where she can provide highly personalized services that build upon each child's unique strengths.

She has a unique way of building relationships with families and communicating with parents in a way that they truly feel like they understand their child. Each child’s goals are written with the entire family in mind, recognizing that there may be some specific communication challenges in the home that need to be targeted during speech therapy sessions.

Billing & Insurance

At this time, Chatter-Roo Pediatric Speech Therapy does not accept insurance. We only accept private-pay clients. A doctor's referral is not needed for an evaluation or services at Chatter-Roo Pediatric Speech Therapy.
